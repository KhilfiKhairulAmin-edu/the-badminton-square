The Badminton Square

The One Stop Information Centre for Badminton

Our mission is to provide knowledge about Badminton sport to students across Malaysia. We believe that sports are not only important towards the achievements of a student, but also affects their physical and mental health. With this website, we believe that we can encourage more students to practice playing this sport as a regular activity and boost their knowledge about Badminton.
